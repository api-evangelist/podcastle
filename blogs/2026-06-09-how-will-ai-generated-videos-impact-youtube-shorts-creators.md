AI-generated videos will make YouTube Shorts faster to produce, easier to remix, and more competitive. For creators, the impact is mixed: AI can lower production barriers and help creators publish more consistently, but it can also increase low-effort competition, impersonation risks, and viewer trust issues. The real question is not only whether AI will replace Shorts creators.

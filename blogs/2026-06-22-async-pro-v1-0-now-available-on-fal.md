We’re excited to announce that Async Pro v1.0 , Async’s flagship multilingual text-to-speech model, is now available on fal . Async Pro is designed for developers building AI voice agents, customer support systems, conversational applications, content creation tools, and localization workflows. The model combines natural speech generation, multilingual support, and advanced text normalization to deliver reliable voice experiences in production environments.

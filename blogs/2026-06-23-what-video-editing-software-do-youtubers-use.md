What editing software do YouTubers use depends almost entirely on their workflow. Professional creators tend to reach for Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve, tools that give them precise control over every cut, color, and audio track. Beginners and short-form creators typically start with something simpler: CapCut, iMovie, Canva, or a mobile editor that gets content out the door fast.

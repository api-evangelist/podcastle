Yes, free AI video generation is absolutely worth it, for the right things. If you want to test an idea, make a short social clip, try turning a still image into motion, play with prompts, or rough out an early draft, a free AI video generator will get you surprisingly far without costing a cent. Where free plans get frustrating is the fine print: watermarks, limited credits, short clip lengths, slower generation, lower export quality, and restrictions on commercial use.
